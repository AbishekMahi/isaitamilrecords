At Isai Tamil Records, we believe that music has the power to connect people and transcend boundaries. We are a music distribution company dedicated to helping independent artists and music producers share their music with the world. With a focus on innovation and creativity, we strive to offer cutting-edge solutions to help our clients succeed in the ever-evolving music industry.

Founded in 2020, Isai Tamil Records has grown to become a leading player in the music distribution industry, with a global reach that spans over 20 countries. We work with a diverse range of artists and genres, from up-and-coming independent acts to established stars.

Whether you're looking to distribute your music to major streaming platforms, monetize your content on YouTube, or build your fanbase through social media and other promotional channels, Isai Tamil Records is here to help. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you grow your music career.

Mission

Our mission at Isai Tamil Records is to empower independent artists and music producers by providing them with the necessary tools, resources, and platforms to succeed in the music industry.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading music label in Indian music industry, known for our personalized support, innovative solutions, and commitment to helping artists achieve their dreams.
